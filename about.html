My name is Steven Ly. aka stevebutu for online name. I was born and raise in San Jose, CA. I always wanted to create video edits and aesthetically pleasing photographys ever since I was a child. I really want to improve my skills and nourish the moments while I progress through these learning phases. I create these types of art because I want the world to know my perspective of whatever I am sharing. Whether it is from a photo or video, I want the person to understand the message that will be displayed through my artwork; complex or simple. With the thought of creating a project and having it published, seems to always push me to put out more artwork.
